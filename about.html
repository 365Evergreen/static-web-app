About 365 Evergreen

We're passionate about helping Australian businesses unlock their full potential through Microsoft 365 and Power Platform solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, 365 Evergreen emerged from a simple belief: every business deserves access to cutting-edge technology solutions that drive real results. Our founders, with over 15 years of combined experience in Microsoft technologies, saw an opportunity to bridge the gap between complex enterprise software and practical business needs.

What started as a small consultancy has grown into Australia's trusted partner for Microsoft 365 and Power Platform implementations. We've helped over 500 businesses transform their operations, improve collaboration, and achieve sustainable growth.

Today, we continue to stay true to our mission: making powerful technology accessible, practical, and transformative for businesses of all sizes.

Our Mission

To empower Australian businesses with Microsoft 365 and Power Platform solutions that drive productivity, innovation, and sustainable growth.
